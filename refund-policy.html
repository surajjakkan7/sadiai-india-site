Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains how refunds and cancellations work for JJ Boutique subscriptions, offered by Sadguru Enterprise. Payments are processed securely through Razorpay.

1. Subscription cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from within the app or by contacting us. Cancellation stops future billing but does not end your current paid period early — you will continue to have access until the end of the billing cycle you have already paid for.

2. Refund eligibility

Because JJ Boutique provides immediate access to AI-generated try-on credits upon payment, subscription fees are generally non-refundable once a billing cycle has started. We make an exception in the following cases:

  • A duplicate payment was charged for the same billing cycle due to a technical error.
  • You were charged but never received access to the paid plan due to a fault on our end.
  • A payment was deducted without your authorization.

3. How to request a refund

To request a refund under the eligible cases above, contact us within 7 days of the charge with your registered email/phone and the payment reference ID from Razorpay. We will review the request and respond within 5 business days.

4. Refund processing time

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method via Razorpay. Depending on your bank or payment provider, this may take 5–10 business days to reflect.

5. Trial or promotional credits

Free trial credits or promotional try-on credits, where offered, have no cash value and are not refundable.

6. Failed or pending payments

If an amount is debited from your account but your subscription is not activated due to a payment gateway error, the amount is typically auto-reversed by Razorpay within a few business days. If it is not, contact us with your payment reference for manual review.
