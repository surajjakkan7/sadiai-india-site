How JJ Boutique works

01

Upload once, try many

A customer's photo is used to generate try-on previews across an entire catalogue of sarees, without a single physical change of clothes.

02

Seven draping styles

Bengali, Gujarati, Kokni, Madisaru, Nivi, Peshwai, and with-dupatta — the app matches regional draping conventions, not just a generic wrap.

03

Built for the shop counter

A kiosk mode runs on a shop's own display, with a front camera for the customer and a second camera for the garment, so try-on happens right at the counter.
