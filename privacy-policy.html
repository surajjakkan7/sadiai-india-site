Last updated: July 2026

sadiaiindia ("we", "us", "our") operates this website and the associated saree virtual try-on application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

  • Photos you upload: used to generate a preview of you wearing a selected saree.

  • Payment information: processed by our payment partner, Razorpay. We do not store card, UPI, or bank details on our own servers.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate saree try-on previews using AI image processing.

3. Photo handling

Photos uploaded for try-on generation are used only to produce your requested preview. We take reasonable technical measures to protect uploaded photos and do not sell or share them with third parties for advertising purposes. Results may be temporarily cached to improve performance on repeat requests.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with payment processors (Razorpay), AI service providers used solely to generate your requested preview, and government or regulatory authorities where required by law.

5. Data retention

We retain account and payment records for as long as your account is active and as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your account and associated photos by contacting us.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. You may also withdraw consent for photo processing, which may limit your ability to use the try-on feature.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
