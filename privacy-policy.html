Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Sadguru Enterprise ("we", "us", "our") operates the JJ Boutique application and this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app or visit this site.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Photos you upload: When you use the virtual try-on feature, we collect the photo you provide to generate a preview of you wearing a selected saree.
  • Account information: Name, phone number, and email address used to create and manage your account.
  • Payment information: When you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by our payment partner, Razorpay. We do not store your card, UPI, or bank details on our own servers.
  • Device information: Basic device identifiers used to enforce one active device per account.
  • Usage data: App usage patterns, such as number of try-ons generated, used to improve the service.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate saree try-on previews using AI image processing.
  • To create and manage your account and subscription.
  • To process payments through Razorpay.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of the try-on experience.

3. Photo handling

Photos uploaded for try-on generation are used only to produce your requested preview. We take reasonable technical measures to protect uploaded photos and do not sell or share them with third parties for advertising purposes. Cached results may be temporarily stored to improve app performance and reduce processing time on repeat requests.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay) to complete transactions.
  • AI service providers used to generate try-on images, solely for the purpose of generating your requested preview.
  • Government or regulatory authorities, where required by law.

5. Data retention

We retain account and payment records for as long as your account is active and as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your account and associated photos by contacting us using the details below.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us. You may also withdraw consent for photo processing, which may limit your ability to use the try-on feature.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app or website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
